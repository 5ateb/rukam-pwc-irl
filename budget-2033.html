📊Executive Summary
The Board-approved 2027-2033 Business Plan projects a total revenue of SAR 363.48 million and cumulative net income of SAR 132.83 million over the 7-year projection period. Operations commence on 1 April 2027 at 75% capacity, reaching full steady-state from 2028 onwards.
