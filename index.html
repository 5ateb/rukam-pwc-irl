Company Overview
Overview
Company Profile

Rukam Industrial Company (formerly "Metal Scrap Recycling Technology Co., Ltd.") is a Saudi limited liability company headquartered in Al Khobar, Eastern Province, operating in industrial recycling. It converts industrial waste into valuable products using advanced, environmentally friendly technologies, primarily producing high-value ferrous metals and premium slag-based aggregates.

  • Year Established: 2021 (commenced operations)
  • Total Assets (31 Dec 2025): SAR 45.54 million
  • Net PP&E: SAR 40.03 million
  • Revenue (FY2024): SAR 16.98 million
  • Revenue (FY2025): SAR 6.23 million
Seven Competitive Strengths
  • Patented Technology: Exclusive partnership with Addforce Switzerland
  • 98% Recovery Rate: Fe metal recovery at 80-90% purity (industry-leading)
  • First-Mover Advantage: Early entrant in Saudi steel slag recycling
  • Proven Operations: Audited track record - SAR 16.98M peak revenue (2024)
  • Strategic Location: Proximity to key steel producers in Eastern Province
  • Circular Economy Alignment: Supports Saudi Vision 2030 environmental goals
  • Scalable Model: Pilot plant validated, main plant construction underway

  • First-Mover Advantage: Early entrant in Saudi steel slag recycling
  • Proven Operations: Audited track record - SAR 16.98M peak revenue (2024)
  • Strategic Location: Proximity to key steel producers in Eastern Province
  • Circular Economy Alignment: Supports Saudi Vision 2030 environmental goals
  • Scalable Model: Pilot plant validated, main plant construction underway
Competitive Landscape

The Saudi steel slag recycling market is nascent with limited direct competition. Rukam's primary competition comes from traditional disposal methods (landfilling) rather than other recycling companies. The Company's technology partnership with Addforce provides significant competitive moat through proprietary processing technology.
