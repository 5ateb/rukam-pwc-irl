General Overview / Company Information
General Information
Company Overview

Rukam Industrial Company is a Saudi limited liability company headquartered in Al Khobar, Eastern Province, operating in industrial recycling. It converts industrial waste into valuable products using advanced, environmentally friendly technologies.

  • Year Operations Commenced: 2021
  • Total Assets (31 Dec 2025): SAR 45.54 million
  • Net PP&E: SAR 40.03 million
  • Revenue (FY2024): SAR 16.98 million
  • Revenue (FY2025): SAR 6.23 million
2025 Revenue Reduction

The reduction reflects a planned, temporary shutdown of pilot plant operations during relocation to comply with Ministry of Industry rules.

Strategic Technology Partnership

Exclusive partnership with Addforce Switzerland providing advanced steel slag processing technology.

Competitive Advantages
  • Patented technology via Addforce Switzerland
  • 98% Fe metal recovery rate (industry-leading)
  • First-mover in Saudi steel slag recycling
  • Audited track record - SAR 16.98M peak revenue (2024)
  • Strategic location in Eastern Province
