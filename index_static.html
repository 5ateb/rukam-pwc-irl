General Overview / Company Information / Business Overview
General Information
Company Overview

Rukam Industrial Company (formerly Metal Scrap Recycling Technology Co., Ltd.) is a Saudi limited liability company headquartered in Al Khobar, Eastern Province, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Operating in industrial recycling, the Company converts industrial waste into valuable products using advanced, environmentally friendly technologies. It currently processes steel slag to recover high-value ferrous metals and produce premium slag-based aggregates for industrial and construction applications.

  • Year Operations Commenced: 2021
  • Total Assets (31 December 2025): SAR 45.54 million
  • Net Property, Plant & Equipment (Including CWIP): SAR 40.03 million
  • Revenue (FY2024): SAR 16.98 million
  • Revenue (FY2025): SAR 6.23 million
2025 Revenue Reduction

The reduction in 2025 revenue primarily reflects a planned, temporary shutdown of pilot plant operations during the relocation to comply with the Ministry of Industry's rules.

Strategic Technology Partnership

Rukam maintains an exclusive technology partnership with Addforce Switzerland, providing access to advanced steel slag processing technology designed to maximize metal recovery, improve product quality, and optimize overall plant efficiency.

Competitive Advantages

Rukam's competitive positioning is underpinned by several strategic strengths:

  • Exclusive access to proprietary and patented steel slag processing technology
  • Industry-leading ferrous metal recovery rates of up to 98%, significantly exceeding conventional recovery methods
  • Production of high-quality recycled metallic products and value-added slag aggregates
  • Strong alignment with Saudi Arabia's circular economy and industrial waste management objectives
  • Strategic location in the Eastern Province with established feedstock supply agreements

Feasibility Study / Budget / Financial Projections
Feasibility & Budget
Feasibility Study History

The Company initially prepared a comprehensive feasibility study in 2016, which was subsequently updated in 2023 to reflect the knowledge and experience gained through market development, operational performance, and the outcomes of its R&D program.

Pilot Plant Validation

The Company successfully designed, constructed, and operated a pilot-scale steel slag processing plant. The pilot plant served as a proof-of-concept facility, validating the proprietary Rukam steel slag processing technology while supporting market development through the production and commercialization of recovered ferrous metals and slag-based aggregates.

2027-2033 Business Plan (Updated July 2026)

The 2027-2033 Business Plan incorporates the key technical, operational, commercial, and financial assumptions for the full-scale industrial processing facility:

  • Technology: Proprietary steel slag processing technology by Addforce Switzerland

Market
7 Competitive Strengths

Competitive Landscape

The Saudi steel slag recycling market is nascent with limited specialized competitors. Rukam benefits from proprietary technology, established supplier relationships, and demonstrated operational capability. The primary competition comes from traditional disposal methods rather than direct recycling competitors.
